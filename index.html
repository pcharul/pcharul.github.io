About Me

I am a Ph.D. scholar working with Dr. Pravesh Biyani in Mobility and Optimization Lab at IIIT-Delhi, India. I am working on providing solutions to reliable public transportation. I have worked on Bayesian Inference, Deep Learning, and Optimization frameworks in transportation problems. I am particularly interested in Statistical Inference for real-world problems.

In my free time, I like to read about psychology and mindfulness.
